ASIA

the holy kabba,saudi arabia

Kaaba, also spelled Kaʿbah, small shrine located near the centre of the Great Mosque in Mecca and considered by Muslims everywhere to be the most sacred spot on Earth. Muslims orient themselves toward this shrine during the five daily prayers, and cherish the ambition of visiting it on pilgrimage, or hajj, in accord with the command set out in the Qurʾān.

THE GREAT WALL ,CHINA

It is a wall extending 2400 km long on the northern and northwestern borders of China. It was built more than two thousand years ago as an integrated defense project. It is one of the Seven Wonders of the World and one of the most important World Heritage sites.

kyoto gardens, japan

The Kyoto area, located south of Honshu Island, is famous for its stunning gardens that usually form part of a temple or historical landmark.

TAJ MAHAL, INDIA

An immense mausoleum of white marble, built in Agra between 1631 and 1648 by order of the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his favourite wife, the Taj Mahal is the jewel of Muslim art in India and one of the universally admired masterpieces of the world's heritage.

AL-AQSA MOSQUE, PALESTINE

Al-aqsa mosque is located in the Old City of Jerusalem in PALESTINE, is the third holiest site in Islam. The mosque was built on top of the Temple Mount, known as the Al Aqsa Compound or Haram esh-Sharif in Islam, several decades after Muhammad's death.

al masjid an nabawi, saudi arabia

is a mosque built by the Islamic prophet Muhammad in the city of Medina in the Al Madinah Province of Saudi Arabia. It was the second mosque built by Muhammad in Medina, after Masjid Quba'a, and is now one of the largest mosques in the world.

ocean park, hong kong

It is a nature marine park that you can get fun for the whole family. Opened in 1977 in the south of Hong Kong Island, it has great attractions and activities.

gardens by the bay,Singapore

It is a nature reserve spanning 101 hectares (250 acres) of reclaimed land in downtown Singapore, adjacent to the Reservoir Marina. The natural complex consists of three waterfront gardens: South Bay Garden, East Bay Garden and Central Bay Garden. The largest of these is the South Bay Park, which occupies an area of 54 hectares (130 acres).

Mount Kinabalu,Malaysia

It is a famous mountain located on the island of Borneo in Southeast Asia, and it belongs to the Malaysian state of Sabah. It is one of the highest peaks of Southeast Asia and attracts adventurers who love to climb mountains.

bali beaches ,indonesia
